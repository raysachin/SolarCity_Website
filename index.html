BUSINESS PROPOSITION

Subscription service

Our application will provide access to premium features, including alert generation, graphical trend analysis, and anticipated generation from our machine learning model, through a subscription-based service.

Maintenance/Cleaning Service

Subsequent to the alert services, encompassing both cleaning and preventive maintenance, will be facilitated through our platform, utilizing a business-to-consumer (B2C) model.

B2B partnership

In pursuit of seamless user experiences and heightened model precision tailored to individual grids, we aim to establish a business-to-business (B2B) partnership. This collaboration seeks to integrate our application with solar panel manufacturers, subject to user consent, enhancing interface usability and model accuracy.

Mechanism for Enhancing Efficiency of Solar Panel

In the present scenario the available Solar panel setup does not allows the user to have an in- depth look into the real-time functioning of the setup. As a result little or no information about the panel functioning / Health, Efficiency Generation is available, Following which the user is Unawar of any maintenance/reppair needed for the panel for its proper functioning.

Through our project we are working in a direction of providing a solution to Solar Panel Mointoring,Solar forecasting/Predicition and Solar Panel Cleaning which could be done by establishing a two point system combining a small IOT Device connected to a Solar Mointoring Application

solar Mointoring Application that is designed to Display not Only the Solar generation at that moment but also the values of Temperature , Humidity ,Dew Point and precipitation(the atmospheric features) that might be affecting the generating as well. Values to all parameter is then utilized to train and test a Machine Learing model which then produces the expected generation. "The expected generation value provide the user with an approximate value of generation based on the present physical/atmospheric conditions or how much gernation is possible at the momment"

The value is then Compared to the actual generation and alerts are generated by the system if uncleaned panels could be possible reason for lower efficieny , the present Weather condition are affecting the generation , or preventive maintenance is needed by solar setup

Mechanism for Enhancing Efficiency of Solar Panel

Our team is dedicated to enhancing the efficiency of solar panels, whether they are being installed or are pre-installed. We recognize that efficiency gains can be achieved through various avenues, including solar panel design, cleaning, monitoring, forecasting, and storage. Given the constraints associated with designing pre-installed solar panels and storage systems, our primary focus throughout our project has been directed towards elevating efficiency through the optimization of Solar Panel Monitoring, Solar Forecasting/Prediction, and Solar Panel Cleaning methodologies. By concentrating on these key aspects, we aim to contribute significantly to the overall effectiveness and performance of solar energy systems, fostering advancements in sustainable energy utilization.

Our dedicated focus on enhancing solar panel efficiency aligns with the strategic initiatives outlined by the Government of India's Ministry of New and Renewable Energy. As of 2021, India has emerged as the fourth-largest global player in solar PV deployment, boasting an impressive installed capacity of approximately 61.97 GW as of November 30, 2022. Notably, the Government of India has set an ambitious target of achieving 280 GW of solar energy, emphasizing the nation's steadfast commitment to expanding its renewable energy portfolio. The current solar energy market in India commands a substantial share, amounting to $38 billion, constituting 69.10% of the broader Renewable Energy market valued at $55 billion.

We have successfully engineered a cutting-edge product designed to generate alerts through the implementation of an advanced two-point monitoring system.
